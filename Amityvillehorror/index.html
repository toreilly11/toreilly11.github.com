The background story

On On November 13, 1974 at 112 Ocean Avenue in Amityville, New York six members of the DeFeo family were shot and killed. The oldest defeo son, Ronalnd Defeo Jr. was the only surviving member of the family and the main suspect in the murders. On October 14, 1975 Ronald went to trial and charged with six counts of murder. Over the years, Ronald has changed his story many times, leading some to bleieve he was not entirely responsible and there was possibly a paranormal factor. On December 18, 1975, the Lutz family moved into the house, According to the family, wihtin a few days of moving in, they allegedly began to experience strange and unexplained events. The true story is still shrouded in mystery and some believe that Ronald Defeo was possessed and the family's home is truly haunted.
